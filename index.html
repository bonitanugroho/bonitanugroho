    About Us

    Founded in 1987 by the Bent brothers, our establishment has been serving up rich taste of steak sourced from french butchers in various regions of South and Central Europe. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best steak, and bringing back to you here in our restaurant. We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last cut. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with family, delighted party with friends or simply just to enjoy some alone time.

    Our Mission

    We serve only the highest quality product, prepare it in a clean environment, and serve it in a warm and friendly manner. We provide our guests with the most fresh modern cuisine with our personal recipes.

    Our Plan

    Guest Focused, Absolute Guest Satisfication, Develop Talent, Operational Excellent, Continuous Improvement, Outstanding Services, Enthusiastic Welcome, Awesome Food, Killer Tunes, and an Unforgettable Time.

    Our Vision

    Make Amaury’s kitchen become the best Belgium Steakhouse. The choices we make about what we eat, where it comes from and how it’s prepared have a direct and powerful impact on individuals and communities.
